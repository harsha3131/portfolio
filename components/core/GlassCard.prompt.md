**GlassCard** — the signature frosted surface; wrap any content that floats over a photographic background.

```jsx
<GlassCard strength="standard" interactive>
  <h3>Project title</h3>
  <p>Short description…</p>
</GlassCard>
```

Strengths: `faint` (subtle, busy backgrounds), `standard` (default), `strong` (modals, hero panels). Set `interactive` to lift on hover. Always needs a photographic / colored background behind it — it has nothing of its own to frost on a flat white page.
